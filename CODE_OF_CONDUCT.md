# Code of Conduct

## Our Standard

This project follows the [Contributor Covenant v2.1](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/).
Participants are expected to engage in good faith, keep feedback specific and actionable,
and treat maintainers' review time as a shared resource.

## Scope

Applies to all interactions in this repository: issues, pull requests, discussions, and
review comments.

## Enforcement

Instances of unacceptable behavior may be reported to the repository owner. All complaints
will be reviewed and investigated, and will result in a response deemed necessary and
appropriate to the circumstances.
